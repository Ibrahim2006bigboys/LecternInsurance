About Our Company

Comprehensive Protection Tailored for You

Lectern Insurance Brokers Limited was incorporated as an Insurance Broking Company under the Companies and Allied Matters Decree of 1990 in December 1993. Our company is registered and licensed to practice all classes of Insurance Consultancy, advisory, and Broking activities, having fully complied with the requirements of the Insurance Act, and a professional indemnity cover of ₦250,000,000.00 (Two Hundred and Fifty Million Naira) with a fully paid-up capital of ₦30,000,000.00 (Thirty Million Naira). Our company is therefore one of the heavily capitalized broking firms in the country.

Our operations are fully IT-compliant and backed by experienced underwriting and claims management professionals. You are therefore guaranteed prompt, efficient, and pro-active broking services. Having conducted insurance business successfully for more than 20 years, we are quite familiar with the local and global insurance market, the expectations of our clients, their obligations, the laws governing insurance as a trade, and the provisions of Nigerian pension laws.

The company is 100% fully owned by Nigerian shareholders, having an authorized capital base of ₦30,000,000.00 (Thirty Million Naira) fully paid, without any foreign ownership participation. Copies of our CAC Form CO2 and CO7 are enclosed for your verification and evidence of registration in Nigeria, as well as our license to operate as an Insurance Broker by the National Insurance Commission (NAICOM), along with our certificate of membership of the Nigerian Council of Registered Insurance Brokers (NCRIB).

Lectern Insurance Brokers Limited markets and provides services in all classes of insurance. We take pride in knowing that for more than 20 years, we have helped our clients find the best coverage at the right prices that suit their needs and expectations. In the event of a claim, we always provide help and counseling to ensure fast and speedy claim settlement.
